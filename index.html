About

I'm a graduate student at the NYU Schack Institute of Real Estate, pursuing an MS in Real Estate Finance & Investment. My academic focus spans CMBS capital markets, REIT portfolio construction, hotel development, and mixed-use residential underwriting.

Before NYU, I built a cross-industry foundation in hospitality operations (including Michelin-starred environments), stadium management, and early-stage real estate development — giving me an operator's eye for how assets perform on the ground, not just on paper.

I combine analytical rigor with real-world pattern recognition. I work fluently in financial modeling, Python-based data analysis, and investment research — and I'm actively building proficiency in institutional tools including ARGUS, CoStar, and Bloomberg.

Originally from Kazakhstan, I've lived and worked across three countries, which shapes my view of global capital flows and cross-border real estate markets.
